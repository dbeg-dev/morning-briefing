⚙️ Automate This at 8am Daily
To send this briefing as an SMS to your phone every morning at 8am:

1. iOS Shortcuts (free, no account needed):
  • Open the Shortcuts app → Automation → New Automation → Time of Day → 8:00 AM → Daily
  • Add action: "Open URL" → paste this URL (bookmark it first)
  • Add action: "Get Contents of URL" (optional, to auto-fetch)

2. Send yourself an SMS automatically:
  • In the same Shortcut, after opening this page, add "Send Message" → To: your number → paste the SMS text
  • Or use IFTTT / Make.com to call this page and forward the output to your phone via Twilio SMS

3. Apple Health data:
  • Each morning, open Apple Health → Summary → copy your sleep & activity stats into the health notes box above before generating
